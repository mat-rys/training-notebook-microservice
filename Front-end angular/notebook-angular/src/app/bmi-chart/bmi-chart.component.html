Calculate Your BMI




BMI is a measurement that assesses
the relationship between a person's weight
and height,and it's used to evaluate
their nutritional status.


BMI Value Ranges:
Less than 16 - underweight
16 - 16.99 - severe thinness
17 - 18.49 - mild thinness
18.5 - 24.99 - normal weight
25 - 29.99 - overweight
30 - 34.99 - obesity class I
35 - 39.99 - obesity class II
40 or greater - extreme obesity

Calculate Your BMR





Your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)
is the number of calories your
body burns at rest to maintain basic
life functions, such as breathing,
circulation, and body temperature.
BMR can be calculated based on weight,
height, age, and gender.
